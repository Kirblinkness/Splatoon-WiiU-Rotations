HOW IT WORKS:

Click the link that has "npdi.cdn.nintendo.net" in it. Make sure you click the link that corresponds with your region of Splatoon!

After downloading, open the byaml in Switch toolbox or any byaml decryptor and export it as an XML.

After exporting, launch Splatoon to see the current rotation.

Once you've seen the current map rotation, look at the Id's listed below, and look through the XML for that exact rotation.

To make sure you have found the current rotation, make sure the rotation you found in the XML matches everyting including

the current Turf/Ranked stages and Ranked mode.

Once you have found the current rotation, You can now look at the future roations! Congrats!
